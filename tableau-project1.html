📊 Hotel Sales Dashboard

📋 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes daily food sales at a local hotel between September 2024 and April 2025. The goal is to identify top-selling items, revenue trends, and opportunities for inventory optimization.

🎯 Objectives

  • Analyze which food items generate the most revenue
  • Identify seasonal or weekly sales trends
  • Recommend menu or pricing adjustments

🗃️ Data Summary

The dataset included daily transactions of 12 food items, with prices ranging from KSh 10 to KSh 100. Data was cleaned in Excel and imported into Tableau.

🖼️ Dashboard

The dashboard was built using Tableau and includes key visualizations like sales trends, item popularity, and cumulative revenue.

🔍 Key Findings

  • Tea and chapati are bestsellers every week.
  • Sales consistently dip on Sundays and holidays.
  • Ugali matumbo and chapati ndengu show strong lunch demand but weaker evenings.

📈 Recommendations

  • Offer bundle deals (e.g., tea + chapati) in the morning hours
  • Run a lunch loyalty program targeting ugali combo meals
  • Reduce low-demand stock on Sundays
