Cafe Sales Dashboard

This dashboard explores café transaction records to uncover insights in item performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and more. It supports strategic decision-making around pricing, inventory, and marketing.

📁 Dataset Overview

The dataset used in this project contains 10,000 transaction records from a café business. It includes:

  • Transaction ID: Unique identifier for each transaction
  • Item: Food or drink sold
  • Quantity: Number of items purchased (float)
  • Price Per Unit: Price of a single item
  • Total Spent: Total price paid (Quantity × Price)
  • Payment Method: Payment type used (Cash, Card, etc.)
  • Location: Branch or outlet where sale occurred
  • Transaction Date: Date of the transaction

The data was cleaned and preprocessed to remove duplicates, handled missing values, and ensure consistent formatting.

📊 Dashboard Features

  • KPI Cards – Total Revenue,Average Sales , Number of Transactions and Average of Quantity sold.
  • Sales Trends Over Time
  • Top-Selling Items
  • Sales by Location
  • Payment Method Analysis

🧠 Business Impact

By visualizing transaction data, café manager can make informed decisions on what items to promote, optimize staffing by hour, and identify best-performing products and payment methods.

🔍 Key Insights

  • Peak Sales Months: February recorded the highest revenue.
  • Top-Selling Item: Salad , sandwich and smoothies were the top 3 most selling products.
  • Payment Trends:Digital Wallet was the most common form of payment
  • High-Spending Customers: More customers preferred in-store spending than take away.
